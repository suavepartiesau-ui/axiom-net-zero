Governance

The Charter.

The Governing Principles of Axiom Net Zero

Article 01 — Inception Balance

Axiom Net Zero is mandated to achieve absolute Carbon Neutrality within its first 365 days of operation. This is not a target we work toward. It is a structural condition of our existence.

Article 02 — Radical Transparency

Every resource aggregated, including solar yield, bio intelligence, and community time, is recorded on an immutable public ledger. Honest accounting is the only way to build trust.

Article 03 — Intergenerational Equity

No choice is made for short term gain. Every resource deployment is measured against its impact on the world our children will inherit fifty years from now.

Article 04 — Decentralised Flow

Resources move from point of origin to point of need with minimal friction. We remove the clutter between nature and families. We do not add to it.

OUR WORK

Resource
Aggregation.

We do not just talk about change. We measure it. Our platform tracks the flow of essential resources across our network of community nodes, ensuring absolute transparency.

NODE 01

Community Solar

Aggregating excess solar yield from local residential clusters and distributing it to community infrastructure and low access households.

NODE 02

Bio Intelligence

Monitoring local biodiversity and air quality in real time. Using that data to direct community led restoration projects where they are needed most.

NODE 03

Collective Time

Aggregating the hours our community spends on restoration and protection. Human output is the most vital resource in our shared ledger.

Community
Engagement.

We believe that restoration is a social act. We deploy resources through community based events designed to promote genuine health and happiness.

ENERGISE RECONNECT SOLTERRIX

Meetups where neighbour-owned energy yield is shared and facilitated via devices, turning technical assets into a unified communal handshake.

COMMUNAL POWER TABLES

A Solterra where communities share electricity over simple food and drink, using neighbourhood yields to fuel the shared kitchen and spark genuine human connection.

WELLNESS IMMERSIONS

Solterrix where voluntary work resource assets are pledged to neighbourhood restoration, creating a visible record of shared purpose and lasting solidarity.
