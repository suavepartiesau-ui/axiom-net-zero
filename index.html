GOVERNANCE

The Charter.

The Governing Principles of Axiom Net Zero

Article 01

Axiom Net Zero is mandated to achieve absolute Carbon Neutrality within its first 365 days of operation. This is a structural condition of our existence.

Article 02

Every aggregated resource — solar yield, biosphere data awareness, voluntary work resource assets — is recorded on an immutable public ledger. Honest accounting is how we build trust.

Article 03

No choice is made for short-term gain. Every resource deployment is measured against its impact on the world our children will inherit in the years to come.

Article 04

Resources move from point of origin to point of need with as little friction as possible. We remove the clutter between nature and families.

OUR WORK

Community Engagement.

We believe that restoration is a social act. We deploy resources through community-based events designed to promote genuine health and happiness.

Energise Reconnect Solterrix

Meet-ups where neighbor-owned energy yield is shared and energy-sharing is facilitated via devices, turning technical assets into a communal handshake.

Communal Power Tables

A Solterra where communities share electricity over simple food and drink, using neighborhood yields to fuel the shared kitchen and local conversation.

Wellness Immersions

Solterrix where voluntary work resource assets are pledged to neighborhood restoration, creating a visible record of communal happiness.
