The Charter.

The Governing Principles of Axiom Net Zero

Article 01 — Inception Balance

Axiom Net Zero is mandated to achieve absolute Carbon Neutrality within its first 365 days of operation. This is a structural condition of our existence.

Article 02 — Radical Transparency

Every aggregated resource — solar yield, bio-intelligence, voluntary work resource assets — is recorded on an immutable public ledger. Honest accounting is how we build trust.

Article 03 — Equity

No choice is made for short-term gain. Every resource deployment is measured against its impact on the world our children will inherit in the years to come.

Article 04 — Easy Flow

Resources move from point of origin to point of need with as little friction as possible. We remove the clutter between nature and families.
